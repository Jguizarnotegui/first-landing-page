About

I started doing some Web Development on my own while learning. In the fall of 2012 I enrolled into Los Angeles Trade Technical College. I enrolled into their Computer Information Systems Program. I earned my Certification and later went on to earn my Associates in Computer Information Systems.

I took classes at other Community Colleges, along with Trade Tech I also attended East Los Angeles College, West Los Angeles College, and Harbor College. I transfered to California State University Los Angeles in the fall of 2017. I graduated there in the Spring of 2019.

Having felt like not enough of the courses needed for me to graduate were based on my field of interest I decided to continue my education on my own and at my won pace.
